Document

<html> is the "root" element, with a lang attribute

<head> contains meta-information about the document, such as its title and character set.

<title> specifies the title of the document, shown in the browser's title bar or tab.

<body> contains the content of the document, including text, images, and other media.

<div> is a generic container for grouping content. Use it to apply styles or layout.

<a> is used to create hyperlinks. The href attribute specifies the link's destination.
